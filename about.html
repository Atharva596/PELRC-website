About PELRC

Professional Education Learning and Research Cell (PELRC)

Our Vision

At PELRC, we aim to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world technical experience. We focus on nurturing innovation, teamwork, and hands-on project development.

Our Mission

PELRC empowers students through internships, guided projects, skill-building sessions, and collaborations with industry professionals to enhance their career readiness.

What We Do

  • Offer real-time internship opportunities with mentorship
  • Promote student-led research and development projects
  • Organize workshops, seminars, and expert talks
  • Encourage collaboration and peer learning
